### 📋 PRD: A simulator for kids to educate them about the order of numbers.

**Product Name:** Number In Line

**Problem Statement:**
A web app simulator to simulate the order of numbers. 

**Solution:**
1. Display a line
2. Get a input from the user - using number pad
3. Displays a real-time simulation from the nest number of the input, high light with those numbers are on front of the input. Then high light those nubmers are after the input. Each at most 10. Start fromt the nestest numbers.

**Example**
Kid enters 42 and presses Enter
App says "forty-two" (or "四十二" in Chinese mode) automatically
App says "on front of forty-two are" (or "四十二前面的是" in Chinese mode), As numbers animate outward (41,40,39,38,37 ...), each is spoken aloud with a short delay
Kid can tap the 🔊 icon on any number to hear it again
Then App says "after forty-two are" (or "四十二后面的是" in Chinese mode), ...
Toggle language switch → voice switches to the other language
Mute button silences all auto-speech (tap-to-hear still works)

**Success Metrics:**
- For kids
- the order
- English wording can switch to Chinese. (a switch for this)
